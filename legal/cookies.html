1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognise your device, remember your preferences, and analyse how our website is used. We comply with the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) 2003 and UK GDPR.

2. Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website and API dashboard to function. They include session tokens, authentication cookies, and CSRF protection tokens. These cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for security and core functionality. No consent is required under PECR for these cookies.

Analytical Cookies

We use first-party analytics to understand how visitors interact with our documentation and marketing pages. Data is aggregated and anonymised. These cookies require your consent.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings such as code language preference in the Developer Hub (Python, Node, Go) and dark/light mode preference.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. The only third-party cookies on our platform are:

  • Stripe.js — Required for PCI-compliant payment processing on checkout pages. Stripe's cookie policy applies.
  • Google Fonts — A session cookie may be set when loading fonts. No personal data is collected.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in or using the API dashboard. You can manage analytical cookie consent via the cookie banner displayed on your first visit.

For more information on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email to registered account holders. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
