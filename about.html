About Us

We're building the infrastructure layer for production AI.

Shok-Intelligence Systems was founded in 2026 by a team of engineers who spent years building prediction systems at scale — and were tired of re-inventing the same infrastructure every time.

Our Mission

Make production AI accessible to every engineering team.

The gap between a data science prototype and a production-grade prediction system costs companies months of engineering time and millions in opportunity cost. We exist to close that gap.

Shok-IS gives any team — from a 3-person startup to a regulated enterprise — the same prediction infrastructure that took Fortune 500 companies years to build in-house.
